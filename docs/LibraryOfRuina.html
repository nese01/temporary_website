library of ruina is lowk my favorite game

It's a thought provoking strategy game that requires a lot of thought and effort put into it

You have to maintain light to do card attacks, do deckbuilding for combat pages while strategizing attributes to put onto your librarian page. It's honestly a whole lot of fun for those who like to think alot. Choosing targets, selecting which speed die, which combat page, which E.G.O page, gathering emotion points, clashing against which targets, all take lots of thought and effort. And especially a lot of reading of enemy passives if you want to win.
